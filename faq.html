CashMate FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new transaction?

Tap the "+" button in the bottom navigation, select Income or Expense, fill in the amount, category, and optional description, then save.

Can I edit or delete transactions?

Yes! Go to the History tab, tap on any transaction, and you'll see options to edit or delete it.

How do I reset my password?

On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password?", enter your email, and check your email for reset instructions.

Is my data secure?

Yes, all data is encrypted and stored securely. Only you can access your financial information.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Manage Data → Clear Transaction Data, then Clear Budget Data. Contact support for complete account deletion.

Can I export my data?

Currently, you can view all your data within the app. Export functionality will be added in future updates.

How do I contact support?

Go to Settings → Help & Support → Contact Support to send us an email.
